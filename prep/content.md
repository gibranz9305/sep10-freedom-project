*Technological Advances*
  
 3D Food Printing
  
   Food Printing uses additive manufacturing to create food by depositing ingredients layer by layer from food-grade syringes and nozzles.
 This Technology allows for more complex and custom-designed foods

Sous Vide Machines

  These machines cooks vacuum sealed food in a tempertaure controlled water bath to a precise temperature. This machine is great as it makes sure food is cooked perfectly and evenly, and can result in enhanced flavors as the bag traps natural juices.

Smart Refrigirators

  These refigirators besides providing basic cooling allow you to remotely change its temperature, give you recipies based on whats in your fridge, entertainment options with touchscreens, and inventory managament.
