# SEP10 Freedom Project
by **X**

## Context
I am a student at [HSTAT](https://www.hstat.org/) in the [Software Engineering Program](https://hstatsep.github.io/). The "Freedom Project" for SEP10 is a year-long project all about making a website that informs the viewer of the current and future innovations in the topic of my choosing.

For my project, I chose the topic of **Cooking**.

I used HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Github. I also chose to independently study **A-frame** in order to help me make my website.

## Implications
##### PROS
* You make a website about any topic you want
* Learn throughout the year
* Engaging
* Present your project at the end of the year infront of many people
* Research what your passionate about
##### CONS
* Work can add up if you procrastinate
* Will be harder if your lazy
* If your shy you need to present
