Cooking

Cooking is prehistoric, as it began with early humans using fire to cook as far as 780,000 years ago. Over time, cooking has become easier with the inventions of many machines and the learning of many cooking techniques to make different foods. This site will look at some cooking technological innovations that make cooking easier and enjoyable, and are used in everyday life.
