Technological Advances

3D Food Printing

Food printing uses additive manufacturing to create food by depositing ingredients layer by layer from food-grade syringes and nozzles. This technology allows for more complex and custom-designed foods.

Sous Vide Machines

These machines cook vacuum-sealed food in a temperature-controlled water bath to a precise temperature. This helps food cook perfectly and evenly, while also enhancing flavor by trapping natural juices.

Smart Refrigerators

These refrigerators provide basic cooling along with features like remote temperature control, recipe suggestions based on what’s inside, touchscreen entertainment, and inventory management.
